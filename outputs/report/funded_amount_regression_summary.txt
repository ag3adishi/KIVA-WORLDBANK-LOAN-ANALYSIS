
📈 Regression Summary: Predicting Funded Amount using Random Forest

This section evaluates the performance of a Random Forest Regressor trained to predict the actual funded amount for loans on Kiva.

📊 Evaluation Metrics:
- Mean Absolute Error (MAE): 1.01
- Mean Squared Error (MSE): 878.67
- R² Score: 0.9993

🖼️ Visualization:
The scatter plot of Actual vs. Predicted Funded Amount shows a tight linear alignment:
- Most points lie close to the diagonal, indicating high prediction accuracy.
- No significant outliers, which suggests minimal overfitting or bias.
- The model is able to generalize well to unseen data.

✅ Conclusion:
The regression model performs with exceptional accuracy and can be considered a reliable estimator for predicting loan funding amounts on Kiva. This regression approach can complement the classification task by adding more granular insights into expected funding.
